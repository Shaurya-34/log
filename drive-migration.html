Things I learned migrating two drives

December 02, 2025 computers

Moved a decade of files across two drives this week. The mechanical part took an evening. The archaeological part took days: every folder a sediment layer of abandoned projects, half-finished courses, and downloads that once felt urgent.

The lesson wasn't about backups. It was that almost nothing needed to survive, and the few things that did were all plain text.
