---
title: Things I learned migrating two drives
date: 2025-12-02
tags: computers
description: File systems, regret, and the archaeology of old projects.
---
Moved a decade of files across two drives this week. The mechanical
part took an evening. The archaeological part took days: every folder
a sediment layer of abandoned projects, half-finished courses, and
downloads that once felt urgent.

The lesson wasn't about backups. It was that almost nothing needed to
survive, and the few things that did were all plain text.
