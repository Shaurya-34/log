---
title: Terminal UIs are underrated
date: 2026-06-28
tags: tools, tui
description: Notes from building with Textual for three months.
---
Three months of building a research tool as a terminal app instead of
a web app. The constraint turned out to be the point: no layout
bikeshedding, no bundlers, and the whole interface is keyboard-first
by default.

The surprise was how much polish is possible inside a character grid.
Reactive layouts, smooth scrolling, focus management. The medium is
limited in exactly the ways that keep a side project shippable.
