---
title: A week with no syntax highlighting
date: 2026-05-09
tags: experiments
description: An experiment in reading code the way you read prose.
---
Turned off every color in the editor for a week. Monochrome tokens,
plain comments, nothing bold. The first two days were genuinely
uncomfortable, like reading without glasses.

Then something shifted: I started reading code linearly, the way you
read prose, instead of letting color tell me what to skip. Structure
had to come from naming and shape. Some of it went back on after the
week ended. Not all of it.
