Terminal UIs are underrated

June 28, 2026 tools, tui

Three months of building a research tool as a terminal app instead of a web app. The constraint turned out to be the point: no layout bikeshedding, no bundlers, and the whole interface is keyboard-first by default.

The surprise was how much polish is possible inside a character grid. Reactive layouts, smooth scrolling, focus management. The medium is limited in exactly the ways that keep a side project shippable.
