A week with no syntax highlighting

May 09, 2026 experiments

Turned off every color in the editor for a week. Monochrome tokens, plain comments, nothing bold. The first two days were genuinely uncomfortable, like reading without glasses.

Then something shifted: I started reading code linearly, the way you read prose, instead of letting color tell me what to skip. Structure had to come from naming and shape. Some of it went back on after the week ended. Not all of it.
